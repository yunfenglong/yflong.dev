# Reliability is part of the portfolio

If I claim I care about engineering quality, availability should be visible. A status page makes that claim measurable.

## What I track

- Service health summaries
- Incident timelines
- Basic system metrics

## Why this matters

A static portfolio often hides operational thinking. By showing uptime and incidents, I can discuss tradeoffs around monitoring, rollback strategy, and blast radius during interviews.

## Cost and complexity

I intentionally kept the dashboard lightweight and static-friendly. The key is signaling engineering intent without introducing unnecessary operational burden.

In short, transparency builds trust faster than polished screenshots.
